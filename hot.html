Controls

  • Rock

    • Click on the Rock bottom,or press the R button on your keyboard

  • Paper

    • Click on the Paper button,or press the P button on your keyboard

  • Scissors

    • Click on the Scissors button,or press the S button on your keyboard

Rules

  • Rock

    • Beats Scissors and looses to Paper

  • Paper

    • Beats Rock and looses to Scissors

  • Scissors

    • Beats Paper and looses to Rock
